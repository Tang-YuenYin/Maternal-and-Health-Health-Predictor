By identifying potential risks in advance, you can take proactive measures, seek appropriate medical advice, and make necessary lifestyle adjustments for a safe and enjoyable journey towards parenthood.

Predictions Features

Harnessing the power of data, our innovative prediction app analyzes baseline value, accelerations, fetal movement, uterine contractions, light decelerations, severe decelerations, prolonged decelerations, abnormal short-term variability, mean value of short term variability, percentage of time with abnormal long-term variability and mean value of long term variability to provide personalized insights into your fetal health risks. With early knowledge and proactive preparation, you can confidently navigate your pregnancy journey, ensuring the best possible outcomes for you and your baby.

  • Baseline Value

    The baseline fetal heart rate is a crucial indicator of fetal health, and deviations from the normal range can signify potential issues that require attention.

  • Accelerations

    Fetal heart rate accelerations indicate the responsiveness of the fetal nervous system and are indicative of fetal well-being.

  • Fetal Movement

    Monitoring fetal movements provides insights into fetal vitality and neurodevelopment. Changes in fetal movement patterns can signal potential concerns.

  • Uterine Contractions

    The frequency and intensity of uterine contractions can affect fetal oxygenation and overall well-being, making it essential to monitor and manage them effectively.

  • Light Decelerations

    Light decelerations in fetal heart rate can occur during contractions and may indicate mild stress on the fetus.

  • Severe Decelerations

    Severe decelerations are significant drops in fetal heart rate and can signal acute fetal distress, requiring immediate medical attention.

  • Prolonged Decelerations

    Prolonged decelerations are sustained decreases in fetal heart rate and may indicate issues such as umbilical cord compression or placental insufficiency.

  • Abnormal Short-Term Variability

    Short-term variability in fetal heart rate reflects the fetal autonomic nervous system's regulation and can be altered in response to fetal hypoxia or distress.

  • Mean Value of Short-Term Variability

    The mean value of short-term variability provides insights into overall fetal heart rate variability and can indicate fetal well-being or distress.

  • Percentage of Time with Abnormal Long-Term Variability

    Abnormal long-term variability in fetal heart rate may indicate fetal hypoxia or neurologic issues and requires careful monitoring and management.

  • Mean Value of Long-Term Variability

    The mean value of long-term variability in fetal heart rate is another important indicator of fetal well-being and neurodevelopment.
